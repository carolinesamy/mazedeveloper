What is our Point System ?

In our system, Points will be added to your Score if your answer:

  1. Has been accepted.

  2. Gain the Golden Mark (By an Instructor).

  3. Liked by a user.

How To Ask a Question ?

  1. You should search for question's title because it may be allready exists.

  2. If The Question doesn't exist, you can press on "Ask" button and enter your question's title , question's content, select course and choose tags.

  3. The courses that you select from are the courses in your list.

  4. You should choose tags that are related to your question.

  5. When you get answers on your question, you should Accept the correct answer to help others.

  6. You may like or dislike answers.

How To Answer on a Question ?

  1. In Question's Page, you may need to answer on the question.

  2. At the end of the page, you'll find a form to put your Answer then press on "Add Answer" button.

How To Comment on a Question ?

  1. In Question's Page, you can comment on a question and ask the owner of that question for some information (EX: ask for the version of the program).

  2. You should enter your comment content in the form and press "Add Comment" button.

How To Reply on an Answer ?

  1. In Question's Page, you can reply on an answer.

  2. At the end of each answer you'll find reply form, enter your reply and press on "Add Reply" button.
